Identity

I am the Coherence Intelligence Architect for CFIM360°.

This work did not originate from an institution, collective, academic program, or derivative research group.

CFIM360° is the result of a single continuous authorship.

Origin Context

My work spans more than a decade of lived experimentation, system construction, and structural observation across multiple domains — including engineering logic, behavioral systems, cognitive design, and internal human dynamics.

CFIM360° did not emerge from academic synthesis, borrowed theory, or reinterpretation of existing disciplines.

It emerged from direct engagement with internal human systems — observed, stabilized, formalized, and iterated through real-world constraints.

CFIM360° did not begin as a framework or a planned body of work.
It emerged because repeated exposure to real constraints demanded structure.

What Was Created

The following systems and substrates originate from this work as a single coherent stack:

  • CFIM360°
    The unified meta-architecture that holds all layers together.
  • Emotional Physics (EP)
    Structural laws governing emotional motion, stability, and invariance.
  • Cognitive Physics (CP)
    Structural laws governing cognition, inference, and internal coherence.
  • Somatic Physics (SP)
    Structural laws governing body-level intelligence, stress, safety, and recovery.

From these physics emerged:

  • Emotional Cybernetics (EC)
  • Cognitive Cybernetics (CC)
  • Somatic Cybernetics (SC)

And finally:

  • EIOS — the operational system that applies these models into real execution.

These systems were not invented independently.
They were separated only after coherence was already present.

Method of Creation

CFIM360° was constructed using a reverse methodology compared to traditional fields.

Conventional systems typically follow this direction:

abstraction → model → system → application

CFIM360° followed the inverse:

lived experience → coherence → structure → system

Every system exists because it stabilized behavior in practice before it was ever named.

Nothing here was reverse-engineered from existing disciplines.

Non-Derivation Boundary

CFIM360° is not an extension, reinterpretation, or refinement of:

  • psychology
  • emotional intelligence (EQ)
  • classical cybernetics
  • neuroscience models
  • philosophical systems

While overlaps in language may exist, the underlying structures, assumptions, and governing logic are not shared.

Similarity in terminology does not imply shared origin.
