Somatic Physics — Case Studies

Identity

This section contains case studies documenting somatic regulation as it manifested in real conditions.

These case studies do not diagnose, treat, or interpret bodily states. They record somatic signaling, stress responses, and stability shifts as they were observed, without therapeutic framing.

The body is treated as a regulatory system, not a subject of intervention.

Reading Guidance

These records should not be read as health guidance.

They do not:

  • recommend practices
  • suggest treatments
  • interpret symptoms
  • assign meaning

They exist to show somatic physics functioning, not bodily improvement.
