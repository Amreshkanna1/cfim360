About CFIM360°

Identity Declaration

CFIM360° (Creative First Impact Model 360°) is a meta-scientific research architecture that formalizes internal human intelligence as coherent, observable, and structurally describable systems.

It models emotional, cognitive, and somatic activity not as abstract experiences or psychological interpretations, but as internally governed systems that operate through structure, coherence, and invariance.

CFIM360° exists as a system independent of interpretation, adoption, or validation.

System Classification and Scope

CFIM360° is classified as:

  • A meta-scientific research architecture
  • A unified inner-intelligence system
  • A non-psychological, non-therapeutic framework
  • A coherence-first system model
  • A field-level architecture rather than a product or methodology

It does not sit within existing academic, psychological, or behavioral disciplines and does not extend or reinterpret them.

Core Domains

CFIM360° operates across three primary internal domains:

  • Emotional Domain
  • Cognitive Domain
  • Somatic Domain

Each domain is treated as a distinct system with its own structure, dynamics, and governing principles.

These domains are unified through a coherence layer that allows cross-domain interaction without collapse into metaphor, narrative, or subjective interpretation.

Physics and Cybernetics Distinction

Within CFIM360°, each domain is articulated through two layers:

  • Physics
    Defines the structural laws and invariants governing internal behavior.
  • Cybernetics
    Defines regulation, feedback, and control mechanisms operating on those laws.

This separation preserves clarity between what governs behavior and how regulation occurs.

What CFIM360° Is Not

CFIM360° is not:

  • A self-help system
  • A psychological framework
  • An emotional intelligence (EI/EQ) extension
  • A motivational or philosophical model
  • A belief system or worldview
  • A metaphorical or narrative construct

It does not aim to persuade, optimize behavior, or provide therapeutic outcomes.

It exists to describe structure.

Origin and Method

CFIM360° was developed through direct lived-system observation followed by long-form structural formalization.

The system did not originate from academic lineage, institutional research, or reinterpretation of legacy models.

Its structure emerged from sustained observation of internal dynamics, coherence behavior, and system invariants under real conditions.

Only after structural stability was achieved were formal models articulated.

Authorship

CFIM360° is authored as a single coherent system.

It is not community-derived, crowdsourced, or incrementally assembled from external frameworks.

All public material reflects a unified internal architecture rather than aggregated perspectives.

Public and Internal Boundaries

Not all components of CFIM360° are publicly exposed.

Some systems operate as internal source layers and are intentionally withheld to prevent misinterpretation, misuse, or partial replication.

Public material reflects only what is structurally safe to publish without leaking operational logic.

System Status

CFIM360° is an active and evolving system.

Public pages represent stable declarations, not the full internal system state.
Absence of material does not indicate incompleteness, but boundary enforcement.

Reading Note

CFIM360° is designed to be read as a system, not consumed as content.

Pages are structured for coherence, not engagement.
Interpretation is secondary to structure.

External Presence

CFIM360 maintains a limited external presence for archival, publication, and reference purposes only.

  • Linkedin — Official company page
  • Instagram — Official account
  • youtube — Official channel

No direct communication, outreach, or individual engagement is conducted through these platforms.
