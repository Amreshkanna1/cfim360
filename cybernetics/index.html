Cybernetics

CFIM360° Cybernetics

CFIM360° Cybernetics defines the regulation layer of the system.

Where Physics establishes structural laws, Cybernetics governs how those laws are stabilized, corrected, and maintained under change.

Cybernetics exists here as internal regulation, not external control.

What Cybernetics Means in CFIM360°

In CFIM360°, cybernetics refers to self-regulating internal systems capable of maintaining coherence under pressure, drift, and disturbance.

It does not describe:

  • machines
  • automation
  • feedback loops in isolation
  • external control mechanisms

Cybernetics here governs living internal systems, not mechanical ones.

Why Cybernetics Follows Physics

Cybernetics cannot exist without physics.

  • Physics defines what is structurally possible
  • Cybernetics governs how stability is preserved within those possibilities

Without physics, regulation becomes arbitrary.
Without cybernetics, structure collapses under real conditions.

CFIM360° treats these as distinct but inseparable layers.

The Cybernetics Domains

Emotional Cybernetics

Governs regulation of emotional motion, overload, equilibrium, and recovery.

Emotional Cybernetics explains how emotional systems self-correct, why suppression fails, and how stability is restored without interpretation or force.

→ See Emotional Cybernetics

Cognitive Cybernetics

Governs regulation of perception, reasoning, bias, and clarity.

Cognitive Cybernetics explains how thinking stabilizes, how confusion compounds, and how cognitive coherence is recovered under complexity and pressure.

→ See Cognitive Cybernetics

Somatic Cybernetics

Governs regulation of bodily signaling, safety states, and stress cycles.

Somatic Cybernetics explains how the body maintains equilibrium, why chronic stress persists, and how recovery becomes possible only after regulation is restored.

→ See Somatic Cybernetics

Integrative Cybernetics

Governs regulation when emotional, cognitive, and somatic systems operate simultaneously.

Integrative Cybernetics addresses system-level coherence, cross-domain feedback, and stability that cannot be achieved by regulating any single domain in isolation.

→ See Integrative Cybernetics

Boundary of This Domain

This section establishes what Cybernetics does and does not contain.

Cybernetics:

  • regulates internal systems
  • maintains stability
  • governs feedback and correction
  • preserves coherence under change

Cybernetics does not:

  • define structural laws
  • provide applications
  • expose interfaces
  • prescribe behavior

Structural definition belongs to Physics.
Execution and manifestation belong to Interfaces.
