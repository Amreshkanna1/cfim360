Somatic Cybernetics

Identity

Somatic Cybernetics defines the control architecture that governs physiological regulation as a constraint system for intelligence and behavior.

It treats the body as a regulatory substrate, not as a signal source for interpretation or meaning.

Purpose

The purpose of Somatic Cybernetics is to formalize how bodily states constrain, modulate, and limit system behavior under real-world conditions.

It does not attempt to improve health or performance. It defines how regulation fails, saturates, compensates, or stabilizes.

Domain of Governance

Somatic Cybernetics governs:

  • Physiological signal modulation
  • Load, fatigue, and recovery constraints
  • Threshold behavior under stress
  • Resource saturation and depletion
  • Cross-layer constraint on cognition and emotion

It does not govern sensation, experience, or subjective awareness.

Structural Properties

Within Somatic Cybernetics, system behavior exhibits:

  • Constraint-driven regulation
  • Non-negotiable thresholds
  • Latent accumulation effects
  • Delayed recovery dynamics
  • Downstream impact on cognitive and emotional control

These properties operate independent of intent or belief.

Failure Modes

Failure in Somatic Cybernetics manifests as:

  • Persistent fatigue states
  • Regulatory bypass under load
  • Collapse of compensatory mechanisms
  • Inability to recover baseline stability
  • Cascading constraint failures across layers

Failure is physiological in origin, systemic in impact.

Boundary Conditions

Somatic Cybernetics does not:

  • Diagnose medical conditions
  • Prescribe treatment or intervention
  • Optimize physical performance
  • Replace medicine, physiology, or sports science
  • Interpret bodily experience

Any such usage constitutes misuse.

Relationship to CFIM360°

Somatic Cybernetics functions as the grounding constraint layer within CFIM360°.

It interfaces with:

  • Somatic Physics (structural origin of bodily regulation)
  • Emotional Cybernetics (signal modulation)
  • Cognitive Cybernetics (decision constraints)

CFIM360° preserves these layers without conflation.

Reading Orientation

  • Articles (operational signals)
  • Case Studies (constraint-driven outcomes)

This page is declarative, not procedural.

System Status

Boundary violations invalidate interpretation.
